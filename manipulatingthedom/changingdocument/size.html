OFFSETWIDTH / HEIGHT THESE TWO PROPERTIES ARE THE SIMPLEST ONES. THEY PROVIDE THE 'OUTER' WIDTH/HEIGHT OF THE ELEMENT. IT TAKES INTO ACCOUNT IT'S FULL SIZE INCLUDING THE BORDERS. CLIENTTOP / LEFT MEASURES THE BORDERS OF THE ELEMENT. CLIENT WIDTH / HEIGHT PROVIDE THE SIZE OF THE AREA INSIDE THE ELEMENT BORDERS. INCLUDES PADDINGS BUT NOT SCROLLBAR SCROLLWIDTH / HEIGHT ALSO INCLUDES THE SCROLLED OUT / HIDDEN PARTS. SCROLL LEFT / TOP ARE THE SCROLLED OUT PARTS ONLY OF THE ELEMENT. ONLY PART THAT IS NOT READ-ONLY, CAN BE MODIFIED.
